Your Questions Answered

Who are we?

We were found in 2020, after the pandemic, to try and help people escape from the boredoom and get in touch with their artistic side.

Can you work with children?

We help create a safe space for your little artists with the help of certified trained assistants that will help your child develop imagination and express through art.

What services do we offer?

We provide our clients with top quality painting kits and trained and recognised hosts, that will guide you in your path to creation and also distressing.
